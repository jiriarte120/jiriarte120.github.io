Introduction

Stella Diver Records

This is a record store specializing in vinyl. The store also sells band merch that can be bought as gifts or with the purpose to be collected.

The target audience will be record collectors and people who prefer to buy cds and records. I'm thinking gen Xers and millenials. People who enjoy concerts are also thought of for customers of this record store.

The people who use this site are the tech savyy millenials looking for convenience.

The primary task is to check the record inventory and the goal would be for the customer to buy records.
